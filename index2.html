Joe Y.F. Lau

Department of Philosophy
The University of Hong Kong

I am currently an associate professor at the Department of philosophy at The Unviersity of Hong Kong. My research areas are in the philosophy of mind and cognitive science, and critical thinking. I obtained my BA in physics and philosophy from Oxford, and received my PhD from MIT. I am the cofounder of Critical Thinking Web, a website providing free online learning resources related to critical thinking, logic, and creativity.
